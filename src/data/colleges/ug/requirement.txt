      <h4>Ghanaian Applicants with WASSCE/SSSCE Qualification</h4>
<br>
<h5>A. General Entry Requirements into the Full-Time Degree Programmes</h5>

<p>An applicant for admission to an undergraduate degree programme in the University of Ghana must have at least credits (A1 - C6 in WASSCE or A - D in SSSCE) in the following subjects: <br>
•	The Four Core Subjects: English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science/Core Science, and Social Studies. <br>
•	Three elective subjects. <br> <br>

</p><h5>B. Calculation of Aggregates for Admission </h5>
The total aggregate of results, comprising six subjects (relevant core and electives), should not exceed: <br>
•	Aggregate 24 for Regular admissions. <br>
•	Aggregate 30 for Distance Education admissions. <br>
<strong> For Science-Related Disciplines:  </strong> <br>
•	Aggregate is calculated using English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science/Core Science, and three elective subjects in the Sciences (refer to specific programme elective requirements below). <br>
•	Note: Social Studies credit is excluded in the calculation. <br>
<strong> For Non-Science Disciplines:  </strong> <br>
•	Aggregate is calculated using English, Core Mathematics, Social Studies/Life Skills, and three elective subjects in General Arts/Business (refer to specific programme elective requirements below). <br>
•	Note: Integrated Science/Core Science credit is excluded in the calculation. <br>

      <div class="view-header">
      <h4>Ghanaian Applicants with WASSCE/SSSCE Qualification</h4>
<br>
<h5>A. General Entry Requirements into the Full-Time Degree Programmes</h5>

<p>An applicant for admission to an undergraduate degree programme in the University of Ghana must have at least credits (A1 - C6 in WASSCE or A - D in SSSCE) in the following subjects: <br>
•	The Four Core Subjects: English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science/Core Science, and Social Studies. <br>
•	Three elective subjects. <br> <br>

</p><h5>B. Calculation of Aggregates for Admission </h5>
The total aggregate of results, comprising six subjects (relevant core and electives), should not exceed: <br>
•	Aggregate 24 for Regular admissions. <br>
•	Aggregate 30 for Distance Education admissions. <br>
<strong> For Science-Related Disciplines:  </strong> <br>
•	Aggregate is calculated using English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science/Core Science, and three elective subjects in the Sciences (refer to specific programme elective requirements below). <br>
•	Note: Social Studies credit is excluded in the calculation. <br>
<strong> For Non-Science Disciplines:  </strong> <br>
•	Aggregate is calculated using English, Core Mathematics, Social Studies/Life Skills, and three elective subjects in General Arts/Business (refer to specific programme elective requirements below). <br>
•	Note: Integrated Science/Core Science credit is excluded in the calculation. <br>

      <h4>Ghanaian Applicants with WASSCE/SSSCE Qualification</h4>
<br>
<h5>A. General Entry Requirements into the Full-Time Degree Programmes</h5>

<p>An applicant for admission to an undergraduate degree programme in the University of Ghana must have at least credits (A1 - C6 in WASSCE or A - D in SSSCE) in the following subjects: <br>
•	The Four Core Subjects: English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science/Core Science, and Social Studies. <br>
•	Three elective subjects. <br> <br>

</p><h5>B. Calculation of Aggregates for Admission </h5>
The total aggregate of results, comprising six subjects (relevant core and electives), should not exceed: <br>
•	Aggregate 24 for Regular admissions. <br>
•	Aggregate 30 for Distance Education admissions. <br>
<strong> For Science-Related Disciplines:  </strong> <br>
•	Aggregate is calculated using English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science/Core Science, and three elective subjects in the Sciences (refer to specific programme elective requirements below). <br>
•	Note: Social Studies credit is excluded in the calculation. <br>
<strong> For Non-Science Disciplines:  </strong> <br>
•	Aggregate is calculated using English, Core Mathematics, Social Studies/Life Skills, and three elective subjects in General Arts/Business (refer to specific programme elective requirements below). <br>
•	Note: Integrated Science/Core Science credit is excluded in the calculation. <br>

      <h4>Ghanaian Applicants with WASSCE/SSSCE Qualification</h4>
<br>
<h5>A. General Entry Requirements into the Full-Time Degree Programmes</h5>

<p>An applicant for admission to an undergraduate degree programme in the University of Ghana must have at least credits (A1 - C6 in WASSCE or A - D in SSSCE) in the following subjects: <br>
•	The Four Core Subjects: English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science/Core Science, and Social Studies. <br>
•	Three elective subjects. <br> <br>

</p><h5>B. Calculation of Aggregates for Admission </h5>
The total aggregate of results, comprising six subjects (relevant core and electives), should not exceed: <br>
•	Aggregate 24 for Regular admissions. <br>
•	Aggregate 30 for Distance Education admissions. <br>
<strong> For Science-Related Disciplines:  </strong> <br>
•	Aggregate is calculated using English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science/Core Science, and three elective subjects in the Sciences (refer to specific programme elective requirements below). <br>
•	Note: Social Studies credit is excluded in the calculation. <br>
<strong> For Non-Science Disciplines:  </strong> <br>
•	Aggregate is calculated using English, Core Mathematics, Social Studies/Life Skills, and three elective subjects in General Arts/Business (refer to specific programme elective requirements below). <br>
•	Note: Integrated Science/Core Science credit is excluded in the calculation. <br>
